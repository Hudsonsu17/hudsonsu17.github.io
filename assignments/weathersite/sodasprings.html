Weekly Newsletter

Bishp Gammon

To all members of the Rexburg YSA 11th Stake, As a Stake Presidency we invite all members of the stake to attend Stake Conference to receive personal revelation on February 23rd and 24th. The theme for the conference is: “Becoming True Disciples of Christ in Higher and Holier Ways”. As part of this conference, the following meetings will be held:Adult Evening Session will be held on Saturday February 23rd from 7pm to 8:30pm. This meeting will be held in the Taylor Chapel. All Stake members are invited and encouraged to attend this session. Leadership Session will be held on Sunday February 24th from 8am to 9:30am. This meeting will be held in SMI 244. Those invited to attend this session include the Stake Presidency, High Council, Bishoprics, Stake and Ward Executive Secretaries and Clerks, Elder’s Quorum Presidencies including Secretaries, Ward Mission Leaders, Stake and Ward Relief Society Presidencies including Secretaries, and Stake and Ward Sunday School Presidencies with Secretaries. General Session will be held on Sunday February 24th from 10am to 12pm. This session is for all stake members and will be held in the Hart Auditorium. Stake Conference is a wonderful opportunity for all members of the stake to come together to be uplifted and edified. We encourage each member to make attending Stake Conference a high priority. As a Stake Presidency we also encourage all to come to Conference with a prayerful heart, ready and prepared to be strengthened as we enjoy the spiritual out-pouring that will bind us together in faith and love for our Savior, Jesus Christ. We look forward to being together with you, Sincerely, The Rexburg YSA 11th Stake Presidency
